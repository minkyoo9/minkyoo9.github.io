Ph.D. Candidate in Electrical Engineering at the Network & System Security (NSS) Lab, KAIST
Daejeon, South Korea
Advisor: Prof. Seungwon Shin

I am a researcher focusing on the safety and security of large language models and data-driven security systems. My work spans model safety (unlearning, backdoors, and safety routing in Mixture-of-Experts models), AI for security, and graph-based analysis for credential abuse. I aim to design scalable, empirically grounded methods that bridge academic rigor with real-world deployment.
